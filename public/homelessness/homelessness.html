My Takeaways

Homelessness is a temporary situation. They're our neighbors who have fallen on bad times, yet they're treated like they're invisible.


I've sat at a traffic light feeling awkward, not knowing what to do or where to look, as a homeless person held up a sign or played a instrument. I was allowing my awkwardness to feed into the abyss of loneliness and invisiblity that homeless people feel.


I refuse to let my awkwardness make someone else feel invisible.
